The Foundational Engine

Explore the mechanistic synergy of the unmoving oral baseline. This section validates how these specific compounds work in tandem to achieve the dual objectives of disrupting cancer metabolism while preserving and rejuvenating healthy tissue.

Methylene Blue: The Metabolic Modulator

At a high dose of 150mg, Methylene Blue acts as a potent alternative electron carrier in the mitochondrial electron transport chain (bypassing Complex I-III dysfunction).

  • ✓
    Anti-Cancer Validation: Reverses the Warburg effect by forcing oxidative phosphorylation, increasing ROS specifically inside dysregulated cancer cells leading to selective apoptosis.
  • ✓
    Rejuvenation Profile: In healthy cells, it acts as a terminal antioxidant, extending cellular lifespan, increasing ATP production, and boosting cognitive function.

NMN: The NAD+ Restorer

Nicotinamide Mononucleotide is critical for replenishing depleted NAD+ pools, which are heavily taxed during cellular stress and aging.

  • ✓
    Synergy with MB: MB increases the rate of cellular respiration, requiring robust NAD+ availability. NMN fuels the sirtuin pathways necessary for DNA repair and genomic stability.
  • ✓
    Skin & Tissue Rejuvenation: Sustained NAD+ levels promote dermal cellular turnover, fighting intrinsic aging and maintaining tissue elasticity.

PEA: The Inflammatory Break

Palmitoylethanolamide (600mg) modulates the endocannabinoid system and directly dampens mast cell activation and neuroinflammation.

  • ✓
    Tumor Microenvironment: Chronic inflammation drives cancer proliferation. PEA alters the tumor microenvironment by downregulating pro-inflammatory cytokines.
  • ✓
    Protocol Tolerance: Mitigates systemic inflammatory responses that can occur during tumor lysis or intense metabolic shifts, preserving host vitality.

Critical Operational Rules & Safety

High-dose Methylene Blue (150mg) is a powerful pharmaceutical intervention. Adherence to these strict biological limitations is required to prevent severe adverse events.

Dietary Tyramine Restriction

Because the 150mg daily dose completely deactivates your gut and systemic MAO-A enzymes, you must adhere to a strict low-tyramine diet to eliminate the risk of a dangerous postprandial hypertensive crisis.

Physiological Monitoring Rules

G6PD Screening Mandate

Because your oncological baseline operates at 150 mg daily, a verified Glucose-6-Phosphate Dehydrogenase (G6PD) status is non-negotiable. Administering this dose with G6PD deficiency will cause rapid, severe red blood cell destruction (hemolytic anemia).

The Urine Tracking Mismatch

Completely ignore the visual color of your urine. High-dose oncological clearance means your urine will remain green or blue far into your washout phases (like Sundays). This is passive terminal elimination and does not reflect active tissue statuses. Rely strictly on the chronological clock.
