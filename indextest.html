Welcome to Gregs

Pennisula and Harbor Handyman Services website

I have been living in the harbor area and working as a handyman since childhood and have carried out a vast range of home improvement projects; from furniture assembly right up to installing new timber decks, pouring concrete, remodeling basement suites and full bathroom renovations. My goal as a self employed handyman is to achieve and maintain a high level of customer satisfaction. I strive to become the best at what I do and maintain a great reputation with my customers. I cover the Harbor and Penninsula areas.

Contact Greg today. Call 310-971-5344 today for a free estimate and references. (Home Base on 14th and Leland)

II'm experienced with Electrical, Plumbing, Drywall, Carpentry, Masonry, and your home repair needs. I can do simple jobs like assemble new furniture from Ikea or Pottery Barn. Some of the most popular services I perform are home repairs such as installing doors and locks, new faucets and garbage disposals, repairing running toilet, repairing drywall, tile work, re-sealing and repair of decks, leaky roofs, weatherproofing, yard work and replacement window installation.
